About Me

Hello! I'm Evan.

I was born and raised in Grand Rapids, Michigan and I recently moved to Littleton, Colorado.
I'm currently working for Crowe as a Product Engineer II.

Recently, I've been expanding my musical skills by learning guitar. I love listening to vinyl and my favorite guitarist is Cory Wong.

Outside of work and music, I love staying active. You can often find me hiking mountains, cycling around town, or enjoying a round of golf.

I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow as a developer, musician, and person. Feel free to connect with me!
